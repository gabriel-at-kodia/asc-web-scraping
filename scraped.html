    Product Overview

    Enhance your curb appeal with EnduraCore exterior composite shutters, built for beauty, strength, and long term performance. Made from a proprietary carbonate reinforced polyurethane composite, these durable shutters are fully weather resistant, standing up to moisture, heat, and cold. Choose from classic louvered, raised panel, and board and batten shutters or explore farmhouse and modern exterior shutter styles to complement any home design. Each shutter features one-piece heavy duty construction and can be custom sized and finished in over 30 styles and 25 fade resistant colors, giving you endless design options for your windows.

    EnduraCore composite shutters can be installed as decorative accents or upgraded with optional SteelTek hardware for authentic, functional operation. Designed as maintenance free exterior shutters for homes, they combine timeless style with the lasting durability homeowners want. Easy to install for professionals and DIYers, EnduraCore shutters are the ideal choice for custom exterior shutters that provide long lasting curb appeal and dependable performance year after year.
    • Standard widths 12" to 18" and heights 24" to 106" or fully custom shutters available
    • EnduraCore is a high-density composite material that is immune to the natural elements like heat, cold, water, and more
    • Shutters will not expand, contract, rot, or warp and come with a lifetime warranty
    • With over 30 shutter designs, both traditional and modern, there is an EnduraCore shutter no matter your home's style
    • Dont see the exact style you want? We can do custom! Contact us today
    • With a similar density to real wood shutters, EnduraCore can be installed using either functional or decorative hardware
